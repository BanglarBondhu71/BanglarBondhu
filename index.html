Our Motivation

Banglar Bondhu is a medical robot which contains 4 different types of controller with a variety of sensor activities(with some basic image detection). It will basically serve the medical purpose by assisting the doctor and helping the nurses. In this coronavirus pandemic time we have faced a very big shortage of our medical personnel(66,958 registered nurses & 86,800 MBBS doctors). Banglar bondhu exactly addresses this point and gives us relief in these cases.
