Our Motivation

Banglar Bondhu is a medical robot which contains 4 different types of controller with a variety of sensor activities (with some basic image detection). It will mostly serve the medical purpose by assisting the doctors and helping the nurses. In this coronavirus pandemic time we have faced a very big shortage of our medical personnel(66,958 registered nurses & 86,800 MBBS doctors). Banglar bondhu exactly addresses this point and gives us relief in these cases.

Specifications

  • Moveable and Flexible hands
  • Display to enable video calling
  • Detect voice and image
  • Has Display, Busket, Freezer, Storing drawer or box, Camera, Speaker, Retina scanner,Table and Stand
  • Sanitizing itself properly, as well as giving other's sanitization
  • Measure temperature , blood pressure, diabetics, oxygen and eye power
  • Shows the result of eye power report on display screen
  • Contains a first aid box and a dustbin attached with his body
  • Collect blood and store in freezer until it can be delivered to the pathology dept
  • Can contact doctors in case of patients emergency condition
  • Uses Machine Learning for predicting diseases from a dataset of some diseases. Here it can detect simple diseases
  • Entertain patients by showing songs,videos and jokes
  • Can provide Video calls with doctors and patients' families
